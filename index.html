About Me.

I am Aryan Gupta, a passionate Web Developer based in Noida, India. With a Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science Engineering from Inderprastha Engineering College (IPEC), I have cultivated a strong foundation in software development and web technologies. My enthusiasm for technology drives me to embrace creativity and solve complex problems, enabling me to deliver innovative solutions in a challenging and rewarding environment.

Throughout my academic journey, I have undertaken various internships that have honed my technical skills and teamwork abilities. My experiences range from Java development to cybersecurity, providing me with a comprehensive understanding of both software engineering and network security. Currently, I am working at Digicorns Technologies, where I focus on developing user-friendly web applications, employing the latest technologies and methodologies.

I have also contributed to over 30 websites, working on significant projects such as The Naked Cupcake, Kaimoving, GPE Home Design, Liveplay, Signage, Smartop, Apexfirein, and Blink High End. My work reflects a commitment to excellence and a passion for creating exceptional user experiences.

I am actively involved in extracurricular activities such as coding, cricket, and dance, which have enriched my leadership and teamwork skills. I am eager to connect with like-minded professionals and contribute to projects that push the boundaries of technology.
